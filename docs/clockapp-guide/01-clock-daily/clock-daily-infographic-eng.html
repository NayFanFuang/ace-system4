ACE ClockApp User Guide

Clock In / Clock Out DAILY

For DAILY employees such as DTA, TE, and Office Staff. Record daily attendance on mobile with GPS and selfie verification.
1

Log In

Enter your email or employee code and password, then tap Login.

2

Check GPS and Clock In

Stay within the allowed area, wait for GPS to show In range, then tap Clock In.

3

Take Selfie Verification

Place your face inside the frame, take the photo, and confirm to save the time record.

4

Clock Out and Check History

At the end of work, tap Clock Out, take the confirmation photo, then review or export from History.

Daily Workflow

  • Open ClockApp on your mobile phone.
  • Log in with your employee account.
  • Allow Location and Camera permissions.
  • Tap Clock In when starting work.
  • Tap Clock Out when finishing work.
  • Check Total Hours and History.

Important Notes

  • GPS must be enabled and you must be within the allowed radius.
  • The selfie photo is saved with each clock-in and clock-out record.
  • If GPS does not appear, wait for signal or re-enable Location permission.
  • If you forget to Clock Out, contact your supervisor or Admin for review.
Clock InUse when starting work. The system records time, GPS location, and verification photo.
Clock OutUse when finishing work. Total working hours are calculated in Today’s Summary.
HistoryReview past records, search, and export CSV when evidence is required.
