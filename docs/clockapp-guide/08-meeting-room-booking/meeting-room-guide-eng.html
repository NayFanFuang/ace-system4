No overlapThe same room on the same date cannot have overlapping active bookings.

Main Menus

  • Schedule: view daily/weekly room availability and select a booking slot
  • My Bookings: review your bookings, edit, cancel, or book again
  • Meeting Summary: summarize room usage across a selected date range
  • Rooms: view room list, capacity, photos, and QR for room display

Quick Troubleshooting

  • Room not visible: clear search or ask Admin to confirm the room is active
  • Time conflict: choose a new time range or switch to another room
  • Invite not received: check attendee/email details, then edit booking to send an update
  • Booking from room display: scan the Room QR or click Book this room on the display
