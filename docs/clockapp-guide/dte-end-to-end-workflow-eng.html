Roles and Responsibilities

  • PM / Project: Assign sites to DTE, monitor Pre-Site, check Pass/Fail
  • DTE: Clock PER_SITE on the correct site, End Site as COMPLETE, upload .rar report
  • System: Sync sites into ClockApp, auto-promote stages, calculate payment

Payment Checklist

  • Site is planned to a DTE and has a completed Clock session
  • Pre-Site is REPORT_DONE, report is attached, and Check Pass has promoted it to ACE_APPROVED
  • If Check Fail occurs, revise the work/report as instructed by PM
  • After payment, the status shows as DTE PAID
