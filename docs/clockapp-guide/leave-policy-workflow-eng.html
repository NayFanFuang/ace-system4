ACE SYSTEM | Leave Policy

Leave Principles & Workflow

Overview of leave types, request rules, approval status, automatic email routing, and the roles of Employee, PM, PD/Head, HR, and Boss.
Use with Leave RequestThis page explains what happens after an employee submits a Leave Request.

Leave Types and Rules

Based on ClockApp / Leave rules

Sick Leave

Default 30 days/year
  • Routes directly to HR
  • HR acknowledges, then status becomes Approved
  • More than 2 days requires supporting document

Personal Leave

Default 3 days/year
  • Submit at least 1 day in advance
  • PM approval completes approval
  • Summary is sent to HR + Boss

Annual Leave

Default 6 days/year
  • Submit at least 3 days in advance
  • PM approval, then PD / Head approval
  • Approved summary is sent to HR + Boss

Other Leave

Policy-based
  • Used when not Sick/Personal/Annual
  • PM approval, then PD / Head approval
  • Checked by reason and supporting documents

Approval Workflow

Sick Leave
Submit→HR Ack→Approved
Personal Leave
Submit→PM→Approved→HR + Boss
Annual Leave
Submit→PM→PD / Head→Approved→HR + Boss
Other Leave
Submit→PM→PD / Head→Approved→HR + Boss

Automatic Email / Notification

On Submit

Sick Leave goes to HR. Personal / Annual / Other goes to PM with related stakeholders in CC.

After PM Approves

Personal Leave becomes Approved and notifies HR + Boss. Annual / Other routes to PD / Head.

After PD / Head Approves

Annual / Other becomes Approved and sends Summary Report to HR + Boss.

On Reject

PM or PD / Head must enter a reject reason. The system emails HR and related stakeholders.

Employee Steps

  • Select Leave Type, Session, Start Date, End Date, and enter Reason
  • Review calculated days, balance, and guidance before submitting
  • Attach supporting documents when required, such as Sick Leave over 2 days
  • Track status in My Leave Requests and cancel while still pending

System Controls

  • Full Day / Morning Half / Afternoon Half are calculated as leave days
  • Overlap checks prevent duplicated leave or attendance records
  • Entitlements are tracked by Sick, Personal, Annual, Other, and calendar year
  • Reject actions require a reason for traceability
